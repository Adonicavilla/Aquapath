Revolutionizing Flood Monitoring and Mobility Solutions

  • About AquaPath

  • Problem & Impact

Aquapath is a real-time flood monitoring system with a re-routing system proposed to help the residents of Barangay Longos to navigate safely during floods which often results in property damage and community disruption. This system takes an innovative method by including real-time environmental data collection, using sensors to monitor flood levels.

By monitoring this, the system is made to provide alerts and insights into flood occurrences, improving response times and relief operations by making all the data available for the community.

Aquapath is designed to improve and enhance the awareness of the community, fostering the safety of the environment and developing the resilience of the community.

Why do we exist? To save lives and keep you moving! Our mission is to create a safer, more mobile Barangay Malolos. Join us in our vision to turn flood fears into flood facts!

Mission

To provide real-time, accurate flood information and safe routing options to the residents of Barangay Malolos, enhancing community resilience and mobility during flood events

Vision

A future where no resident of Barangay Longos is caught off-guard by flooding. We envision a community that moves confidently, regardless of weather conditions

Values

Safety, Innovation, Community Engagement, and Environmental Awareness drive our efforts to create a more resilient Barangay Malolos.

Problem Statement

  1. Flood Risk - Barangay Malolos faces frequent flooding due to its low-lying areas and proximity to water bodies.

  2. Mobility Issues - Unexpected floods disrupt daily life, impeding travel to work, school, and essential services.

  3. Information Gap - Lack of real-time flood data leaves residents unprepared for sudden inundations.

Impact

Floods suck, but we don’t! Our system improves safety and mobility, ensuring you can navigate your community without a kayak. Let’s turn those flood risks into a thing of the past!.
